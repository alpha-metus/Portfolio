    My Other Projects

    OS in Browser

    In this project, I utilized HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to develop a fully functioning operating system interface. I implemented various features, including the ability to add new windows, restart the system, and shut down the interface. Additionally, I integrated an iframe option into the project to enhance its functionality.

    EchoBrief

    In this project, I created a website named EchoBrief, which serves as an AI-powered meeting summarizer. EchoBrief uses advanced artificial intelligence algorithms to analyze and summarize the contents of online meetings, providing users with concise notes and summaries. This innovative solution aims to enhance productivity by streamlining the process of capturing key points from meetings, allowing users to focus on more important tasks.

    Ai Aura

    For this project, I made an introductory page for a website named AI Aura. The purpose of AI Aura is to serve as a one-stop destination for users to access a variety of AI tools. The intro page was designed to provide a clear overview of the available AI tools, their features, and the benefits they offer. My aim was to create an intuitive and user-friendly interface that allows users to easily navigate and find the AI tools that best suit their needs.

    CSV Langchain AI

    In this project, I made CSV LangChain, utilizing Python, OpenAI, and LangChain technologies. This innovative software allows users to upload CSV or PDF files and ask specific questions related to the content, such as requesting a summary or notes. The software then employs advanced AI algorithms to process and analyze the uploaded documents, subsequently providing accurate and insightful answers to the user's queries. CSV LangChain aims to enhance the efficiency of data analysis and information retrieval.
